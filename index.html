ABOUT EWALD

The EWALD MUSIC is a gospel performing arts Industry composed of Christian young people from different denominations in Cameroon. We use our talents in different areas of music and performing arts to evangelize and worship God Almighty. Through gospel music and performing arts events organized by the industry in different denominations and different parts of Cameroon, we are able to bring our vision to the light. The group also has the burden to raise up gospel ministers in the domain of music and performing arts thus equipping these ministries in the church.
